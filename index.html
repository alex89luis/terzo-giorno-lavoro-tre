Focaccia with Caramelized Onion,
Cranberrizer,and Brie

   

Wow ypur taste with this festive focaccia appetizen topped with sweet caramelized onion,
tart cranberries,and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

 

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teanspoons istant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350h) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
   
    For the topping
  • 3 medium yellow onion,thinly siliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil,for brusc-hing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paringknife if desired,thought it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cup fresh or frozen cranberries
 

Method

 

1.Make the focaccia dough

 

In a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together
all the dough ingredients until the flours is fully absorbed. You dont' need to actually knead
the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to
incorporated the last of the flour...
